Security notes

Protecting private access.

These notes explain security monitoring, user responsibilities, and how to report a concern without publishing sensitive implementation details.

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Authorised access

Protected pages and features are available only to authorised users with a valid session. Access may be refused, revoked, or restricted where authentication fails or a request appears unsafe or abusive.

Security information

Sign-in and protected requests may record IP address, timestamp, user agent, requested page, authentication outcome, challenge result, failed-login reason, rate-limit event, and related security information. This is used to authenticate users, detect abuse, investigate faults, and protect private services.

Limited alerts about successful, failed, or blocked sign-in attempts may be sent through a third-party notification service. The Privacy Policy identifies relevant recipients and explains retention and rights.

Automated checks

Automated checks may validate sessions, apply rate limits, require a challenge, or allow or block a request based on authentication and security signals. They protect private areas and are not used to profile ordinary public visitors or make legal or similarly significant decisions about them.

Cloudflare Turnstile

The sign-in page uses Cloudflare Turnstile to help prevent automated abuse. Turnstile processes browser and request signals under Cloudflare's applicable privacy terms.

Your responsibilities

  • Keep login details, one-time codes, and sessions private.
  • Do not attempt to bypass authentication, challenges, rate limits, or access controls.
  • Sign out on shared devices and clear site cookies if a device is lost or no longer trusted.
  • Report unexpected sign-in, session, upload, AI, or protected-app activity promptly.

Reporting a security issue

Send security concerns to mason@mas0ng.com. Include enough information to understand and reproduce the issue, but do not include passwords, active session values, API keys, or unnecessary personal data.

Please allow reasonable time to investigate before publicly disclosing an unresolved issue. Do not access, alter, retain, or share data that is not yours.
