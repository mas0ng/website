Cookie notice

Cookies and browser storage.

This notice explains the limited cookies and browser storage used by public pages, sign-in, and site tools.

Last updated: 22 June 2026

Public pages

Public pages do not use cookies or similar technologies for analytics, advertising, behavioural tracking, or tracking across websites.

After you select “Okay”, informational public pages store one local value so the privacy notice stays dismissed. It does not identify an account or record browsing for advertising or analytics.

The privacy notice

Selecting “Okay” stores an acknowledgement in local storage so the notice does not appear on every visit. The button acknowledges the notice; it is not consent to advertising or analytics, and no such tracking is used.

Sign-in cookies

Successful sign-in sets essential authentication and short-lived verification cookies. They are used to keep a browser signed in, verify access to protected areas, and protect the sign-in process. Public pages may contact the authentication service with any existing session cookie to determine whether the browser is already signed in; an anonymous check does not create a session.

These cookies are treated as strictly necessary because protected services cannot be provided securely without them.

Cloudflare Turnstile

The sign-in page uses Cloudflare Turnstile to prevent automated abuse. Turnstile may access cookies, browser information, and security signals needed to provide the challenge. This processing is used only where required to protect authentication.

Tools and protected apps

Some tools store information in local or session storage when needed for their features, such as timer state, temporary console output, interface preferences, or draft state. Protected apps may also hold temporary browser state while a feature is in use. This information stays in the browser unless the relevant feature says it will be uploaded or saved to an account.

No analytics or advertising storage

The current public pages do not set analytics, advertising, or behavioural tracking cookies. If that changes, this notice and the site's controls will be updated and consent will be requested where UK law requires it.

Your controls

You can clear cookies, local storage, and session storage through your browser settings. Clearing authentication cookies signs you out. Clearing functional storage may reset the privacy notice or tool state.
