Cookie notice

Cookies and browser storage.

This notice explains how mas0ng.com uses cookies, local storage, and similar browser technologies across public pages and private apps.

Last updated: 25 May 2026

Essential cookies

Protected areas use essential session cookies to keep a signed-in browser authenticated and to verify access to private pages and APIs. These are treated as strictly necessary because the protected service cannot be provided securely without them.

Local browser storage

Public pages may store a small local value after the privacy notice has been seen. Private app interfaces may use browser state for UI preferences, draft inputs, selected models, temporary upload state, or similar local functionality. Under PECR, local storage is treated like other storage and access technologies, so non-essential uses should be explained and consented to where required.

AI app storage

The AI app stores chats and uploads server-side in the configured Worker/R2 storage, not only in browser storage. The browser may temporarily hold selected files before upload and may display attachment state during a chat session.

Analytics and advertising

The current public pages do not load third-party analytics scripts, advertising pixels, or behavioural tracking scripts. If non-essential analytics or advertising storage is added later, it should not be set before valid consent is obtained.

Consent changes

If the site adds non-essential cookies, analytics, advertising, or similar tracking later, the notice and controls should be updated so users can make a clear choice before those technologies are used.

Managing cookies

You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Clearing essential session cookies will sign the browser out of protected areas and may clear local preferences.
