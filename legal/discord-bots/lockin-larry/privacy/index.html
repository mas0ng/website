LockIn Larry

Privacy Policy.

This policy explains how LockIn Larry handles Discord details, schedules, tasks, activity records, and AI-assisted messages.

Last updated: 11 July 2026

Back to LockIn Larry policies

Who is responsible

LockIn Larry is operated by Mason through mas0ng.com. For UK data protection purposes, Mason is the controller of personal data handled specifically to operate the bot. Privacy questions and requests can be sent to mason@mas0ng.com.

Data the bot handles

  • Discord identifiers needed to recognise your user, server, channel, or interaction.
  • Bot commands and messages you send to the bot, plus the bot's responses.
  • Schedules, reminder preferences, tasks, routines, workout details, completions, progress, and other information you choose to track.
  • Basic operational data such as timestamps, error details, and security or abuse-prevention logs.

Do not provide sensitive personal information that the bot does not need. In particular, task and workout tracking is not intended to hold medical records or emergency information.

How the data is used

Data is used to respond to commands, deliver scheduled encouragement, guide tasks, remember your settings and progress, show activity history, maintain and secure the bot, diagnose faults, and prevent misuse.

The main lawful basis under UK GDPR is legitimate interests in providing, maintaining, and securing the bot. Where you ask the bot to perform a specific service, processing may also be necessary to provide that service. You can stop using the bot and request deletion as described below.

Local task storage

Your task, routine, workout, completion, and progress records are stored by LockIn Larry and are not sent to external analytics, advertising, or cloud storage services. They are used to provide the bot's tracking and guidance features, except where disclosure is required by law or needed to protect the service and its users.

Where data comes from and what is required

Data comes from you and other Discord users through commands and messages, from Discord interaction metadata, and from operational events produced when the bot runs. Discord identifiers and command content are required to respond and associate saved tasks with the correct user or server. If they are not made available, the relevant bot feature cannot work.

DeepSeek AI processing

LockIn Larry uses the DeepSeek API for AI-assisted messages. The text needed to generate an AI response may be sent to DeepSeek for processing. Stored task or progress records are not sent to DeepSeek unless they are deliberately included as context needed to answer your request or guide the task. See DeepSeek's privacy policy.

Do not include confidential or sensitive personal information in prompts. DeepSeek acts as a separate service provider and may process API requests according to its own terms, privacy information, retention practices, and processing locations.

Discord processing

Because LockIn Larry operates on Discord, Discord processes messages, interactions, account information, servers, and channels on its platform under Discord's privacy policy and terms. This policy covers LockIn Larry's handling of data after it is made available to the bot; it does not control Discord's separate processing.

Sharing

Personal data is not sold and is not used for advertising. It may be shared only with Discord as the platform, DeepSeek when an AI response is requested, infrastructure providers strictly needed to operate the server, or authorities and other parties where legally required or necessary to protect rights, safety, and service security.

Retention and security

Task records and preferences are kept while needed to provide your bot features. Operational and security logs are kept only for as long as reasonably needed to investigate faults, prevent abuse, and protect the service. Data may be deleted earlier when you request deletion or stop using the service, unless it must be kept for a legal or security reason.

Reasonable technical and organisational measures are used to restrict access. No internet-connected service can guarantee absolute security.

Your rights and choices

Depending on the circumstances, UK data protection law may give you rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, object to processing of, or obtain a copy of your personal data. You can also ask to clear tracked tasks and progress, change reminder settings, or stop future messages.

Send requests to mason@mas0ng.com and include your Discord user ID so the relevant records can be identified. Identity may need to be verified before a request is completed. You may also complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office.

International processing and automated output

Discord, DeepSeek, and infrastructure providers may process information outside the UK. Where UK data protection law requires a transfer safeguard, the transfer must use adequacy regulations, approved contractual protections, or another permitted mechanism. You can ask for information about applicable safeguards using the contact address above.

AI-assisted messages may be generated automatically, but the bot does not use them to make solely automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about users.

Children

LockIn Larry is not directed at children below the minimum age required to use Discord in their country. Do not use the bot if you are not permitted to use Discord.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when bot features, service providers, or legal requirements change. Material changes will be reflected here and the date above will be updated.
