AI policy

Using private AI features.

This policy explains what is processed when an authorised user sends prompts or files to an AI feature.

Last updated: 11 July 2026

What is processed

AI features may process prompts, selected conversation history, model settings, filenames, file content, extracted text, generated images, responses, and usage information. Only submit content that you are permitted to process.

AI providers

Prompts and relevant conversation context are sent to the provider selected for the request, currently OpenAI or DeepSeek. Those providers process the content to generate a response and may process it outside the UK under their applicable terms, privacy information, product settings, and retention arrangements.

Uploaded files and text extraction

Readable text files may be processed to provide attachment context. Images and PDF files may be sent to OpenAI to extract visible text, even if DeepSeek or another provider is selected for the later conversation. Extracted text may then be included with a prompt sent to the selected provider.

A file may still be stored and shown as attached when its contents cannot be extracted. Do not upload confidential, sensitive, or third-party material unless it is necessary, lawful, and appropriate to send to the relevant providers.

Storage and retention

Chats and files may be saved so they can be reopened. Deleting a chat removes its saved chat and attached files from mas0ng.com. Files are retained for pinned chats and the five most recent unpinned chats by default; files from older unpinned chats may be deleted while chat text remains. Temporary chats are not saved by mas0ng.com after the tab is closed. AI providers may keep their own service records under the applicable account settings and terms.

Lawful basis and rights

Processing is based mainly on legitimate interests in providing and securing the requested private feature. Legal obligations may apply in limited circumstances. Information about recipients, international processing, retention, and UK data protection rights is in the Privacy Policy.

Limits of AI output

AI responses can be inaccurate, incomplete, biased, outdated, or unsuitable. Review important output before acting on it. Do not rely on AI output as legal, medical, financial, security, or other professional advice, or as the sole basis for a high-impact decision.

AI output is not used by mas0ng.com to make solely automated decisions that produce legal or similarly significant effects about public visitors or authorised users.

Do not submit or request

  • Passwords, API keys, authentication codes, or other secrets.
  • Unnecessary health, financial, identity, or other sensitive personal data.
  • Personal data about another person without a lawful reason and permission.
  • Illegal, harmful, abusive, exploitative, or rights-infringing material.
