Cookie Notice.

This notice explains how mas0ng.com uses cookies, local storage, and similar browser technologies.

Essential cookies

Protected areas use essential session cookies to keep a signed-in browser authenticated and to verify access to private pages.

Local browser storage

The public page may store a small local value after the privacy notice has been seen so the same message does not need to appear every visit.

Analytics and advertising

The current public pages do not load third-party analytics scripts, advertising pixels, or behavioural tracking scripts.

Managing cookies

You can clear cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Clearing essential session cookies will sign the browser out of protected areas.
