Privacy Policy

Last updated: 08 May 2026

01. Overview

mas0ng.com is a static personal profile site. You can browse the site without creating an account and without accepting analytics tracking. Optional analytics are off by default and only run if you choose "Allow analytics" in the privacy banner.

02. Who Is Responsible

The person responsible for this website is Mason. For privacy questions, consent changes, or data rights requests, email mason@mas0ng.com.

03. Information This Site May Process

The site does not collect account details, passwords, payment information, or profile forms. If you contact me by email, Signal, or a linked platform, I use the information you send only to read, respond to, and manage that conversation.

The site stores your privacy choice locally in your browser using local storage. This is used only to remember whether you chose to keep analytics tracking off or allow analytics.

04. Cookies, Local Storage, And Analytics

Non-essential analytics are off by default. The Google Analytics tag is not loaded unless you explicitly opt in by pressing "Allow analytics." If you choose "Keep tracking off," analytics remains disabled and this site attempts to clear existing Google Analytics cookies such as _ga, _gid, and related _ga_* cookies.

If you opt in, Google Analytics may set cookies or similar identifiers to help show aggregate information such as visits, pages viewed, approximate location, device/browser type, and referral source. I use this to understand whether the site is working well. I do not use analytics for advertising, personalised ads, or selling data.

You can change your choice at any time using "Privacy Choices" in the sidebar. Withdrawing consent stops future analytics from this site and attempts to remove existing analytics cookies from your browser.

05. Legal Basis

Optional analytics use consent as the lawful basis. Site delivery, security, and basic technical operations rely on legitimate interests, such as keeping the website available, secure, and functioning correctly. Direct messages you send are processed to respond to your request.

06. Third-Party Services

The site links to external platforms including TikTok, Instagram, GitHub, Signal, Google, and email services. External links show a warning before opening. Once you leave mas0ng.com, the privacy policy of the external service applies.

If you opt in to analytics, Google may receive analytics data and may process it outside the UK or EEA under its own privacy and transfer safeguards. Google Analytics is not loaded by this site unless you opt in.

07. Cloudflare And Security Processing

Certain interactive features on this website may submit data through secure endpoints powered by Cloudflare Workers. These lightweight server-side functions are used to process specific actions such as retrieving structured data used by parts of the site.

Cloudflare may process limited technical information such as IP address, request metadata, browser details, and security logs to deliver the site, prevent abuse, and protect the network. This is infrastructure processing and is separate from optional analytics consent.

08. Retention

Contact messages are kept only for as long as needed to handle the conversation or any follow-up. Browser privacy choices stay in local storage until you change them, clear site data, or use a different browser/device. Google Analytics cookies, if you opt in, may last for up to two years unless you withdraw consent or clear them sooner. Aggregate analytics reports are kept only for site performance review and are not sold or used for advertising.

09. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict, or object to the use of your personal data. You can also withdraw analytics consent at any time. To make a request, email mason@mas0ng.com.

If you are in the UK and are unhappy with how a privacy request is handled, you can contact the Information Commissioner's Office at ico.org.uk.

10. Updates And Contact

This policy may be updated when the site changes or when privacy rules change. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
