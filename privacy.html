Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 FEB 2026

01. Overview

Welcome to mas0ng.com. Your privacy is important. This policy outlines how any information provided via social links or direct contact is handled, as well as how analytics are processed. Essentially: I use basic analytics to see how the site performs, but absolutely no tracking happens unless you explicitly agree.

02. Information Collection

This website is a static portfolio and does not require user accounts. I do use cookies for analytics, but only if you explicitly grant permission through the cookie banner. Any direct information shared with me (via Signal, LinkedIn, etc.) is used solely for the purpose of our communication.

03. Third-Party Services

My site contains links to external platforms (TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, Signal). Once you leave mas0ng.com, the privacy policies of those specific platforms apply. I am not responsible for the data collection practices of these third parties.

04. Analytics

I use Google Analytics to monitor site traffic and understand user interactions. To ensure your privacy is strictly respected, this process is managed by CookieYes. Analytics scripts and cookies are blocked by default and will only run if you explicitly opt-in via the cookie consent banner. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected, sold, or shared with advertisers.

05. Contact

If you have questions regarding your data or this policy, feel free to reach out via Signal or any of my linked social platforms.
