Welcome to mas0ng.com. Your privacy is important. This policy outlines how any information provided via social links or direct contact is handled. Essentially: I don't track you, but the platforms I link to might.

03. Third-Party Services

My site contains links to external platforms (TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, Signal). Once you leave mas0ng.com, the privacy policies of those specific platforms apply. I am not responsible for the data collection practices of these third parties.

04. Analytics

I may use simple, privacy-focused analytics to monitor site traffic (like view counts), but no personally identifiable information (PII) is stored or shared with advertisers.

05. Contact

If you have questions regarding your data or this policy, feel free to reach out via Signal or any of my linked social platforms.
