Lab Diagnostic

How to play:

Analyze experimental lab data for a mystery substance (melting point, boiling point, electrical conductivity). Deduce its bonding structure.

Why it helps:

Edexcel constantly tests your ability to identify substances purely from data tables. This trains that exact skill.
